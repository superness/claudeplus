## Revenue Model Summary

**Business Model**: Open-core SaaS with subscription-based snapshot infrastructure

**Pricing Tiers**:
- Free: $0 (full software, self-hosted)
- Starter: $10/month (1 coin, snapshot sync)
- Pro: $25/month (5 coins, priority support)
- Enterprise: $100+/month (custom)

- Blended CAC: $12.50 (community-driven)
- LTV:CAC ratio: 25:1 (Year 1), 18:1 (Year 5)
- Payback period: ~1.1 months
- Gross margin: 73-80%

**Sensitivity Analysis**:
- Best case: $5.4M ARR by Year 5
- Base case: $3.1M ARR by Year 5
- Conservative: $1.45M ARR by Year 5
