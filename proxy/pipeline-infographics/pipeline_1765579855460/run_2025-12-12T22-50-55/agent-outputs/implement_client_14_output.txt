## Implemented
**Basic Combat System** with the following features:
- **Melee Attack**: SPACE key triggers a 180° frontal arc attack with 50px range
- **Attack Damage**: 70 damage per hit (matching Choppa Boy's "Chop" skill)
- **Attack Cooldown**: 0.5 second between attacks
- **Visual Feedback**: Golden attack arc shows swing direction, enemies flash red when hit
- **Damage Numbers**: Floating damage text appears on hits
- **Enemy Death**: Enemies fade out and scale up on death (60 HP each)
- **Enemy Contact Damage**: 8 DPS when enemies are within 40px range
- **Player HP**: 100 HP with color-coded HP bar (green→yellow→red)
- **Player Death**: "YOU DIED" screen with R to respawn
- **Player Rotation**: Character sprite rotates to face movement direction
- **UI Updates**: HP bar, HP text, attack cooldown indicator

## How to Test
1. Open `client/index.html` in a browser (or serve via `npx http-server client`)
2. Move with WASD/Arrow Keys - player rotates to face direction
3. Press SPACE to attack - golden arc appears showing attack range
4. Walk toward enemies in the eastern chamber (right side of map)
5. Face an enemy within 50px and press SPACE - enemy takes 70 damage (shown as floating text)
6. Gretchin have 60 HP, so one hit kills them
7. Let enemies touch you to take damage (2 damage every 250ms = 8 DPS)
8. Die and press R to respawn at starting location
