üìù Lessons Learned

Evidence Over Promises

The review process consistently rejected plans that promised to "show" or "provide" evidence without specifying HOW. The winning plan included concrete verification steps and specific validation methods.

Confidence Calibration

"Medium confidence" wasn't acceptable without robust validation. The final plan acknowledged uncertainties honestly while providing a clear path to verify correctness through existing examples.

Discovery Before Design

Several attempts were rejected for being "discovery plans" rather than validated designs. The successful approach committed to research-first methodology with clear evidence collection.

Functional Over Structural

Creating a syntactically valid JSON wasn't enough. The final plan demonstrated understanding that the pipeline must actually WORK, not just exist as a well-formed file.
