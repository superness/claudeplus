Root Cause Identified

The condition in restoreState() uses broken boolean logic:

if (p.isKing === state.pieceIsKing ||
    (p.x !== state.pieceX || p.y !== state.pieceY))

This || (OR) logic matches almost every piece on the board instead of the specific moved piece, causing cumulative board corruption during AI move evaluation.

client/game.js Lines 1029-1031
