# XIII. COMBAT DESIGN SUMMARY

## Core Design Pillars

1. **Skill Over Stats**: Builds and player skill matter more than gear level
2. **Build Diversity**: 10 classes × secondary access = hundreds of viable builds
3. **Tactical Depth**: Positioning, timing, and team coordination decide battles
4. **Risk/Reward**: High-risk options (Psykers, Weirdboys) offer high returns
5. **Faction Identity**: Each faction feels distinctly different in combat
6. **Integration**: Combat feeds into and draws from economy/progression smoothly

## Balance Validation Points

- ✅ TTK consistent across level ranges (4-6 seconds for standard enemies)
- ✅ Class roles distinct but not rigidly enforced
- ✅ Resource generation matches economy targets
- ✅ Difficulty modes provide appropriate challenge scaling
- ✅ Loot progression meaningful without being mandatory
- ✅ Bad luck protection prevents frustration
- ✅ Consumable economy slightly positive (allows stockpiling)
