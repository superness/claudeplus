# Economy Systems Design Document
## Warhammer 40,000: Imperium Divided - Damocles Gulf Sector

---

# I. ECONOMY PHILOSOPHY

Drawing from Guild Wars 1's economy design, this system prioritizes **meaningful player choice over grind-based accumulation**. The economy serves gameplay, not the reverse. Trading should connect players, not isolate them behind auction interfaces.

**Core Principles:**
- Player-to-player trading as primary economy driver
- NPC vendors as floor/ceiling price anchors, not primary commerce
- Regional variation creates travel-based arbitrage opportunities
- Faction identity extends to economic behavior
- Inflation controlled through meaningful sinks, not artificial caps

# II. MARKET SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE

## A. Trading Hub Structure

```
TRADING HUB HIERARCHY

SECTOR CAPITAL (Damocles Prime Station)
├── Cross-Faction Auction House
├── Bulk material trading
├── Legendary item marketplace
├── Currency exchange (faction currencies)
└── Access: All factions, requires standing

FACTION HUB (One per faction)
├── Faction-only Auction House
├── Faction-specific vendor
├── Crafting station cluster
├── Reduced listing fees (faction members)
└── Access: Faction members only

REGIONAL OUTPOST (8-12 per faction territory)
├── Local vendor (limited stock)
├── Trade board (player postings)
├── No auction house
├── Direct player-to-player only
└── Access: Open

FRONTIER POST (Contested zones)
├── Black market vendor (premium prices)
├── Cross-faction player trading
├── High-risk, high-reward items
├── No fees (lawless zone)
└── Access: Anyone brave enough
```

## B. Trading Methods

### 1. Direct Player-to-Player Trading
```
DIRECT TRADE INTERFACE

Features:
├── Both players must be in same zone
├── Real-time trade window
├── Up to 10 items + currency per side
├── Confirmation countdown (5 seconds)
├── Trade logging (dispute resolution)
└── No fees

Security Features:
├── Item hover shows full stats
├── "Verify Rarity" button (confirms item tier)
├── Currency cap display
├── Modified item warnings
└── Scam report system
```

### 2. Auction House System
```
AUCTION HOUSE MECHANICS

Listing Options:
├── Buyout Only: Fixed price, instant purchase
├── Auction: Bidding with minimum + buyout option
├── Seeking: "Want to buy" postings
└── Bundle: Multiple items, single listing

Listing Duration:
├── 24 hours: 3% listing fee
├── 48 hours: 5% listing fee
├── 72 hours: 7% listing fee
└── Maximum 10 active listings per character

Sales Tax: 10% of final sale price
├── 8% removed from economy (sink)
├── 2% to faction treasury (visible community fund)

Search Features:
├── Filter by: Type, Tier, Stats, Price range
├── Sort by: Price, Time remaining, Relevance
├── Watchlist functionality
└── Price history graphs (30-day)
```

### 3. Trade Boards (Regional)
```
TRADE BOARD SYSTEM

Purpose: Connect buyers/sellers for direct trades
├── Post "Want to Sell" or "Want to Buy"
├── Contact info (character name)
├── No automated transactions
├── No fees
└── Creates regional marketplaces

Posting Limits:
├── 5 active posts per character
├── 48-hour auto-expire
├── Repost cooldown: 1 hour
└── Bump feature: 100 gold
```

### 4. Mail Trading
```
MAIL TRADE SYSTEM

Features:
├── Send items + currency via mail
├── Recipient can accept or return
├── C.O.D. option (Cash on Delivery)
├── Cross-character (same account): Free
├── Cross-account: 50 gold per mail
└── Maximum 5 items per mail

C.O.D. Mechanics:
├── Sender sets price
├── Recipient pays to receive items
├── 24-hour accept window
├── Auto-return if not accepted
└── 5% C.O.D. fee (sink)
```

## B. Dynamic-Price Vendors

### Regional Supply Vendors
```
DYNAMIC PRICING FORMULA

Base Price × Supply Modifier × Demand Modifier = Current Price

Supply Modifier:
├── Abundant (>200% stock): 0.7x price
├── Normal (80-200% stock): 1.0x price
├── Low (40-80% stock): 1.3x price
├── Scarce (<40% stock): 1.8x price
└── Depleted (0%): Not available

Demand Modifier (player purchases in 24h):
├── Low (<50 purchases): 0.9x price
├── Normal (50-200): 1.0x price
├── High (200-500): 1.15x price
└── Extreme (>500): 1.4x price

Price Update: Every 4 hours
Price Memory: Trends toward baseline over 72 hours
```

MATERIAL VENDOR

## B. Crafting Cost vs. Sale Value
```
CRAFTING PROFITABILITY FORMULA

Crafting Cost = Materials + Vendor Fee + Time Investment
Expected Sale = Market Price - Auction Fee

Profit Margin Target: 15-30% for common items
Premium Margin: 50-100% for excellent rolls
Loss Scenario: Poor rolls sell at or below material cost

Example (T3 Weapon):
├── Material Cost: ~350 gold equivalent
├── Crafting Fee: 35 gold
├── Total Cost: 385 gold
├── Average Sale: 500 gold
├── Auction Fee: 50 gold
├── Net Profit: 65 gold (17% margin)
└── Excellent Roll: 750-1000 gold sale (70%+ margin)
```

# VIII. PLAYER TRADING PROTECTIONS

## A. Anti-Exploit Measures

```
TRADE SECURITY SYSTEMS

Real-Money Trading Prevention:
├── Unusual gold transfers flagged for review
├── New accounts: Trade limited to 500g/day for 7 days
├── Large trades (>10k) require 24h hold
├── Cross-faction trades monitored
└── IP correlation tracking

Market Manipulation Prevention:
├── Purchase limits: 50 of same item per day
├── Listing limits: 10 active auctions per character
├── Price spike detection: Items >300% average flagged
├── Coordinated buying detection (same guild/IP)
└── Artificial scarcity prevention (relisting same item)

Scam Prevention:
├── Trade window shows full item stats
├── Modified items highlighted in red
├── Currency verification display
├── 5-second confirmation countdown
├── Report system with investigation queue
└── Repeat offenders: trade ban escalation

# IX. INFLATION CONTROL MECHANISMS

## A. Dynamic Sink Adjustment

```
INFLATION MONITORING SYSTEM

Tracked Metrics:
├── Total gold in circulation
├── Average player wealth by bracket
├── Material prices vs. baseline
├── Transaction velocity
└── Gold generation vs. sink ratio

Automatic Adjustments (if inflation detected):

Mild Inflation (5-10% above baseline):
├── Repair costs +10%
├── Waypoint costs +15%
└── Vendor prices +5%

Moderate Inflation (10-20% above baseline):
├── Repair costs +25%
├── Waypoint costs +30%
├── Auction fees +2%
└── New gold sinks activated (cosmetic vendors)

Severe Inflation (>20% above baseline):
├── Emergency vendor sales (rare items for high gold)
├── Limited-time sink events
├── Temporary generation reduction
└── Developer intervention review
```

## B. Structural Inflation Prevention

```
BUILT-IN PREVENTION MECHANISMS

Currency Binding:
├── Requisition Tokens: Character-bound
├── Faction currencies: Account-bound within faction
├── Event currencies: Time-limited
└── Intent: Multiple currency channels

Material Consumption:
├── Crafting consumes materials permanently
├── No full-recovery salvaging
├── Consumables consumed on use
└── Equipment degradation (repairs)

Gold Velocity Control:
├── Auction fees remove gold from system
├── NPC purchases are permanent sinks
├── No gold duplication vectors
└── Trade taxes at every step

Natural Decay Systems:
├── Ork Teef: 1% weekly decay
├── Event currencies: Expire after events
├── Reputation currencies: Use-it-or-lose-it events
└── Intent: Encourage spending over hoarding
```

├── Event Cosmetics: High cost, prestige
└── Event Exclusives: Very high cost, limited supply

Post-Event:
├── Unspent currency converts to gold (50% rate)
├── Event items become tradeable after event
├── Prestige items retain value
└── Next year: Different items (old ones remain rare)
```

# XI. COMBAT REWARD INTEGRATION

## A. Combat to Economy Pipeline

```
COMBAT REWARD → ECONOMY FLOW

Enemy Kill:
├── Direct gold drop: 10-50% of value
├── Material drop: 30-60% chance per enemy
├── Equipment drop: 5-15% chance (scales with enemy)
├── Consumable drop: 10-20% chance
└── Faction token: 5% chance (elite enemies)

Total Expected Value Per Enemy:
├── Standard enemy: 15-30 gold equivalent
├── Veteran enemy: 40-75 gold equivalent  
├── Elite enemy: 100-200 gold equivalent
├── Boss enemy: 300-800 gold equivalent
├── World boss: 1000-5000 gold equivalent
```

# XIII. SUCCESS METRICS

```
ECONOMY HEALTH INDICATORS

Healthy Signs:
├── Player trading active (>60% of transactions)
├── Stable material prices (±15% weekly variance)
├── Gold sinks operating at 65-75% absorption
├── New players reaching T2 gear in <5 hours
├── Auction house listings turning over in <48 hours

Warning Signs:
├── Deflation: Players hoarding, not spending
├── Inflation: Prices rising >10% weekly
├── Market Stagnation: Listings not selling
├── Wealth Gap: Top 10% holding >50% of gold
├── Bot Activity: Unusual farming patterns

Intervention Thresholds:
├── 3 consecutive weeks of warning signs
├── Single extreme metric violation
├── Player reports exceeding baseline 2x
└── Cross-reference with retention data
```
