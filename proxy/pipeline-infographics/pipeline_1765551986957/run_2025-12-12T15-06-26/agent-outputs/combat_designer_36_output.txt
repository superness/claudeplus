## Design Philosophy Achieved:

**Guild Wars 1 DNA Preserved:**
- Skill bar limitations force meaningful choices
- Build diversity matters more than gear score
- Secondary professions enable creativity
- No gear treadmill - skill matters

**Warhammer 40K Identity Maintained:**
- Faction combat feels distinct (Zeal vs Focus vs WAAAGH!)
- Grimdark tone in abilities and enemy design
- Lore-appropriate mechanics (Perils of the Warp, Markerlight, etc.)
- Scale from horde fights to elite encounters

**Balance Analyzer Concerns Resolved:**
- All 5 critical exploits have documented fixes
- Dominant strategies bounded by cap systems
- Weak options buffed to viability
- New systems prevent future exploits
