The combat system demonstrates strong foundational design with Guild Wars 1-inspired build diversity and tactical depth. Core DPS calculations are mathematically sound, with mid and endgame targets properly achieved.

**Critical Issues Found:**
- Tau Markerlight stacking creates 65% damage bonus (should cap at 32%)
- Ork WAAAGH! scales exponentially with group size (needs hard cap)
- CC immunity can be gamed for near-invincibility
- Crisis Suit Nova Reactor bypasses intended risk/reward
