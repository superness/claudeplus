# VIII. BALANCE CORRECTIONS SUMMARY

## Addressing Previous Balance Analysis Issues

| Original Issue | Solution Implemented |
|----------------|---------------------|
| Enemy HP scaling off by 10-15× | HP values increased 5-15× across all tiers. Bosses now 15,000-40,000 HP. |
| WAAAGH! stack reaches +200% damage | Hard cap at 10 stacks (+50% max). Decay system added. |
| Markerlight stacks to +120% damage | Max 5 stacks per target (+40% max). Party-wide cap of 5 active. |
| Supporting Fire chain exploit | Max 1 trigger per ally attack. 5s internal cooldown. |
| Permanent CC chains possible | CC Immunity Windows: 10s after any hard CC on Elite+ enemies. |
| Plasma self-damage easily negated | Not addressed in this document (equipment design territory). |
| 3 classes have no defined abilities | Sanctioned Psyker, Kroot Carnivore, and Ethereal now fully designed. |

## New Balance Safeguards

```
ANTI-EXPLOIT SYSTEMS

1. BUFF STACKING CAP
   └── Maximum 2.0x total damage from buffs (multiplicative)

2. FACTION STACKING PREVENTION
   └── Max 2 of same class per party
   └── Diverse party XP bonus (+5% per unique class)

3. CC IMMUNITY WINDOWS
   └── 10s immunity after hard CC on Elite+
   └── Soft CC reduced to 50% during immunity

4. BOSS DAMAGE CAPS
   └── Cannot take >5% max HP from single hit
   └── Prevents one-shot build exploitation

5. ENRAGE TIMERS
   └── All bosses enrage at 15 minutes
   └── Prevents infinite slow-and-safe strategies
```
