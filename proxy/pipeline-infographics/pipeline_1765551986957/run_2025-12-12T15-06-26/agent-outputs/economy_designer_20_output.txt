# X. COMBAT INTEGRATION

## A. Combat Reward Structure

```
COMBAT → ECONOMY FLOW

Enemy Kills:
├── Direct gold drops (scaled by tier)
├── Vendorable loot (gear, junk)
├── Materials (crafting economy)
├── Consumables (usage or sale)
└── Faction tokens (specialty vendors)

Kill Reward Scaling:
Enemy Tier | Gold Drop | Material Chance | Consumable Chance
-----------|-----------|-----------------|------------------
T1 Trash   | 5-15g     | 60% T1, 15% T2  | 10%
T2 Standard| 10-25g    | 50% T1, 25% T2  | 15%
T3 Veteran | 20-40g    | 40% T2, 15% T3  | 20%
T4 Elite   | 35-60g    | 40% T2, 20% T3  | 25%
T5 Champion| 50-100g   | 30% T3, 15% T4  | 30%
Boss (tier)| See IX.A  | Guaranteed      | 35%
```

## B. Progression Cost Integration

```
PROGRESSION COSTS BY TIER

Tier 1 → Tier 2 Transition:
├── Equipment upgrade cost: ~1,500g total
├── Skill unlocks: ~500g (5 skills)
├── Consumable budget: ~200g
└── Total transition: ~2,200g (achievable by level 15-20)

Tier 2 → Tier 3 Transition:
├── Equipment upgrade cost: ~6,000g total
├── Skill unlocks: ~1,500g (specialized skills)
├── Consumable budget: ~800g
├── Mount: ~2,000g (optional but recommended)
└── Total transition: ~10,300g (achievable by level 30-35)

Tier 3 → Tier 4 Transition:
├── Equipment upgrade cost: ~25,000g total
├── Skill unlocks: ~4,000g (elite skills)
├── Consumable budget: ~2,000g
├── Prestige items: ~15,000g (optional)
└── Total transition: ~46,000g (achievable by level 50-55)

Tier 4 → Tier 5 Transition:
├── Equipment upgrade cost: ~80,000g total
├── Skill unlocks: ~8,000g (mastery skills)
├── Consumable budget: ~5,000g
├── Prestige items: ~40,000g (expected at this level)
└── Total transition: ~133,000g (achievable by level 70-75)

Tier 5 → Legendary Transition:
├── Equipment crafting: ~120,000g (8 pieces @ 15,000g each)
├── Raid materials: Time-gated (4-6 weeks per piece)
├── Mastery unlocks: ~15,000g
├── Ultimate prestige: ~200,000g (aspirational)
└── Total: ~335,000g + time investment
```
