## Feature Complete: Warhammer 40K MMO Game Client Prototype

### What Was Built
A fully functional 3D game client prototype for a Warhammer 40K MMO with Guild Wars 1-style gameplay, including:

- **3D Third-Person Camera System** - Orbital camera with zoom (5-30 units), right-click rotation, smooth follow
- **Guild Wars 1 Skill Bar** - 8 regular skill slots + 1 elite slot with cooldown overlays
- **Warhammer 40K Visual Theme** - Grim dark aesthetic with Imperial red/gold, gothic architecture, atmospheric particles
- **Movement System** - Click-to-move + WASD with collision detection and wall sliding
- **Collision System** - Buildings and pipes with proper player collision
- **UI Framework** - Character portrait, health/energy bars, minimap, chat box, skill tooltips
- **World Environment** - Ground plane, sky dome, gothic buildings, pipes, atmospheric effects
