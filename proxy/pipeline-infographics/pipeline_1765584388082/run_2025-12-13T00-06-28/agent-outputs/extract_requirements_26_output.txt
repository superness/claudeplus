**Skill Acquisition - From progression-design.md:**

**Skill Tier System (points required for tree progression):**
- Tier 1: 0-5 points in tree
- Tier 2: 6-15 points in tree
- Tier 3: 16-25 points in tree
- Tier 4: 26+ points in tree

**Skill Point Schedule by Level:**
- Level 1-5: 1 point per level (5 total)
- Level 6-10: 2 points per level (10 total)
- Level 11-15: 2 points per level (10 total)
- Level 16-20: 1 point per level (5 total)
- Level 21-25: 0.5 points per level (2-3 total)
- Level 26-30: 0.5 points per level (2-3 total)

**Elite Skills - From progression-design.md:**
> "Elite skills are powerful abilities obtained through specific content."
> "Each class has 8-10 elite skills available."
> "ACQUISITION METHODS:
> Boss Drops (50% of elites):
> ├── Defeat specific bosses on Hard+ difficulty
> ├── 10% base drop rate, +5% per difficulty tier
> ├── Account-bound, not tradeable
>
> Reputation Vendors (30% of elites):
> ├── Requires max reputation with specific faction
> ├── Costs 200-500 Requisition Tokens
>
> Quest Chains (20% of elites):
> ├── Complete specific story arc
> ├── Usually 3-5 connected quests"

**Elite Skill Costs:**
- Learning: 10 skill points (one-time)
- Equipping: Occupies elite slot on bar
- Limit: 1 elite skill equipped at a time
- Swapping: Free, but requires out-of-combat
