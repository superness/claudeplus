AI Opponent

A complete minimax-based AI system with alpha-beta pruning that plays as Black. The AI evaluates board positions, considers material advantage, positional bonuses, and handles multi-jump sequences intelligently.

  • Minimax algorithm with depth limiting
  • Alpha-beta pruning for performance
  • Position evaluation (center control, advancement)
  • Multi-jump sequence handling
  • 500ms delay for natural feel

Drag-and-Drop UX

Intuitive piece movement replacing the confusing click-to-select then click-to-move pattern. Players can now drag their pieces naturally to their destination squares.

  • Mouse drag event handling
  • Touch support for mobile devices
  • Visual drag feedback (enlarged piece, shadow)
  • Cursor changes (grab, grabbing, pointer)
  • Smooth piece following cursor
