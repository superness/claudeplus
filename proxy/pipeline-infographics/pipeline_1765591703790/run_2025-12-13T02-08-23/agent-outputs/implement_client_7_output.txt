All systems have been implemented. Here's a summary of what was built:

## Completed Implementation

### 1. Companion System (`/client/src/game/companions/`)
- **CompanionTemplates.ts**: 18 Henchmen (6 per faction) + 7 Heroes with exact skill bars
  - Imperial: Militant Elena, Crusader Marcus, Medicae Sofia, Tech-Adept Cog, Storm Trooper Rex, Psyker Mordecai
  - Tau: Shas'la Kais, Shas'ui Fireblade, Earth Caste Healer, Pathfinder Shadowstrike, Broadside Pilot, Ethereal Acolyte
  - Ork: Gutsmasha, Shoota Git, Painboy Snikrot, Weirdboy Zogwort, Flash Git, Kommando Sneakgit
- **Companion.ts**: 3D entity with faction-colored procedural models, health/energy bars
- **PartyManager.ts**: Max 4 party (1 player + 3 companions), zone-based henchman limits
- **CompanionAI.ts**: 0.5s tick rate, 5-priority decision tree (Survival > Support > Control > Damage > Positioning)

### 2. Enemy Threat Tiers (`EnemyThreatTiers.ts`)
- **Minoris** (White): 80-150 HP, 15-25 DPS - Fodder enemies
- **Majoris** (Blue): 180-350 HP, 35-55 DPS - Standard threats
- **Extremis** (Yellow): 400-800 HP, 60-100 DPS - Elite enemies
- **Terminus** (Purple): 2000-8000 HP, 120-200 DPS - Mini-bosses
- **Apex** (Gold): 8000-20000 HP, 200-400 DPS - Raid bosses

### 3. Combat Validation (`/client/src/combat/CombatValidation.ts`)
- Range checking, line of sight via THREE.js Raycaster
- Energy cost validation, cooldown tracking
- Status effect blocks (stun, silence, knockdown, fear)

### 4. Party UI (`/client/src/ui/PartyUI.ts`)
- GW1-style party frames with health/energy bars
- Role indicators (Tank/Healer/DPS/Support/Control)
- Hero vs Henchman styling, stance controls

### 5. Tactical Mission System (`/client/src/game/missions/TacticalMission.ts`)
- 3-phase structure with checkpoints and wave compositions
- Difficulty scaling: Normal, Hard, Veteran, Nightmare
- Sample missions per faction with themed objectives

The client-side systems are ready for integration with the existing backend.
